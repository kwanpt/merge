What’s all this with Web Components?

Ever heard of Web Components? Probably yes. It’s the new unicorn for Webdevs. Is it so? What’s happening in the community? Let’s take a look at projects like Polymer and X-Tag, but first understand the vanilla W3C standards that allow this to happen: HTML Imports, Shadow DOM, HTML Templates and Custom Elements. A talk with João Almeida (Microsoft)

Break

Wow, great talk right? But let me go see the amazing view from the balcony!

Creating a great Engineering Culture

Roundtable with Peter Tilsen (Uniplaces), João Quitério (Talkdesk), João Pedro Martins (CreateIT) and Rui Ribeiro da Silva (GrandUnion). What makes great teams? How much autonomy and transparency is right? Why would you join some teams and not others? Featuring four speakers from different backgrounds, we'll discuss these and other topics in a very interactive session.

Networking (and more drinks!)

Introvert or extrovert, don't be shy, be chatty, get to know people! Instagram the event, take selfies, go wild!
